Band

Hands like houses

Hands Like Houses is an Australian rock band from Canberra. Formed in 2008, the group came together from from the remnants of other local bands and quickly found their groove together. Their debut album, Ground Dweller, was released on 13 March 2012 and charted at number 141 on the Billboard Top 200 and number 2 on the Billboard Heatseekers Chart.

In January 2012 the band signed with Rise Records and released Ground Dweller in March, streaming the entire album on the Alternative Press website. Ground Dweller debuted at No. 141 on the Billboard Top 200 and number 2 on the Billboard Heatseekers Chart. They have since been on the rise, signed to Hopeless records, and continue to tour the world, with their latest single, "Sick", reaching No. 34 on the US Main Rock Chart.
